    • About Me

    Hi there!
    My name is Naina. I graduated from the University of California, Davis in 2022 with Bachelor's degrees in Cognitive Science and Linguistics. Currently, I am the Social Media and Web Design Manger at a company based in Mountain View called Aerendir Mobile.
    In January, I started taking classes at West Valley College to get formal experience in Design. From ages 9-15 I took a traditional art class, where I learned how to sketch, draw and paint in different mediums. I started using Photoshop around the age of 12, and I have been interested in design ever since. I decided to take an HTML/CSS class to better my understanding of the syntax, but I really enjoy the design aspect to web development the most! Eventually I hope to become a UX/UI Designer!

    In my free time, I like to make make jewelry and do my skincare and makeup! While in college, I was knee-deep in linguistics and psychology research, and I sought a creative outlet. So, in the height of lockdown, I developed an interest in skincare, and in 2021 I bought makeup for myself for the first time. I found these hobbies as an escape from an otherwise stressful time. Now, I still love to collect skincare and makeup products! A dream goal of mine is to develop my own cosmetics line!
